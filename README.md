# 🌍 CarbonLens AI

> AI-Powered Sustainability Intelligence Platform

CarbonLens AI helps users measure, understand, and reduce their carbon footprint through personalized analytics, AI-driven recommendations, predictive insights, and engaging gamification.

## 🚀 Features

* 🌱 Carbon Footprint Calculator
* 🤖 AI Sustainability Insights
* 📊 Interactive Analytics Dashboard
* 🎯 Personalized Reduction Goals
* 🏆 Achievements & Badges
* 🔥 Daily Streak System
* 📈 Sustainability Score Tracking
* 📄 Smart Environmental Reports
* 🌍 Lifestyle-Based Recommendations
* ♿ Accessibility-First Design

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Most people want to reduce their environmental impact but lack a personalized and engaging way to understand where their emissions come from and how to improve. CarbonLens AI transforms sustainability into an actionable and rewarding journey.

## 💡 Solution

CarbonLens AI combines carbon footprint analysis, AI-powered recommendations, predictive analytics, and gamification to help users make smarter environmental decisions and build sustainable habits.

## 🛠️ Built With

* HTML5
* CSS3
* JavaScript
* Chart.js
* Local Storage APIs
* AI-Powered Insight Engine

## 🌎 Vision

Measure. Understand. Reduce. Sustain.
